UNDERTALE

Every choice matters.

Independent Game Creator

UNDERTALE is an indie RPG created by Toby Fox. It is known for its emotional storytelling and its unique system that allows players to choose mercy over violence.

Game Features

A unique combat system that combines turn-based battles with bullet-hell mechanics.

Story Highlights

Every choice affects the story. Your actions determine the ending you will reach.

Gameplay

Players can fight, act, or show mercy. Sometimes, not fighting is the best choice.
